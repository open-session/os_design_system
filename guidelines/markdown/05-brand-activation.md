# Brand Activation Guidelines

> How Open Session shows up in the world — from web to social to physical spaces.

---

## Personality

Our brand personality comes through in how we present ourselves and our team.

### Showing Human

We believe in showing the humans behind the brand.

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Real Faces** | Use actual team photos, never AI-generated |
| **Authentic Moments** | Capture genuine interactions, not posed shots |
| **Diverse Representation** | Reflect the diversity of our community |
| **Behind-the-Scenes** | Show the work process, not just outcomes |

### Showing Flaws

Perfection is not the goal — authenticity is.

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Work-in-Progress** | Share iterations, not just finals |
| **Honest Struggles** | Acknowledge challenges openly |
| **Learning Publicly** | Document what we're figuring out |
| **Humble Confidence** | Expert knowledge with openness to being wrong |

### Personality Traits

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| **Curious** | Always exploring, never settled |
| **Generous** | Share knowledge freely |
| **Grounded** | Practical over theoretical |
| **Warm** | Approachable, never cold or corporate |
| **Direct** | Clear communication, no jargon |

## Web Presence

How we show up on our digital properties.

### Website Principles

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Speed First** | Performance is a feature |
| **Content Forward** | Substance over flash |
| **Clear Navigation** | Users find what they need |
| **Accessible** | AAA contrast, keyboard nav, screen readers |

### Page Hierarchy

| Page Type | Purpose | Tone |
|-----------|---------|------|
| **Homepage** | First impression, value prop | Confident, inviting |
| **Product Pages** | Feature explanation | Clear, benefit-focused |
| **Blog/Resources** | Education, thought leadership | Helpful, expert |
| **About/Team** | Human connection | Warm, authentic |
| **Contact** | Conversion, support | Welcoming, direct |

### Visual Treatment

- **Hero Sections**: Full-bleed imagery with Charcoal or Vanilla overlays
- **Typography**: Display headlines, readable body text
- **Imagery**: Real photography over stock, authentic over polished
- **Animation**: Subtle, purposeful motion using brand tokens

### Color Application

| Element | Color Rule |
|---------|------------|
| **Backgrounds** | Vanilla or Charcoal only |
| **Text** | Inverse of background |
| **CTAs** | Aperol accent (sparingly) |
| **Borders** | Never brand colors |

## Social Media

Channel-specific activation guidelines.

### Platform Strategy

| Platform | Purpose | Content Focus |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| **Instagram** | Visual inspiration | Work samples, process, team |
| **Twitter/X** | Thought leadership | Industry insights, hot takes |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional network | Case studies, hiring, company news |
| **Dribbble** | Design community | Polished work, design details |
| **YouTube** | Education | Tutorials, talks, behind-scenes |

### Content Pillars by Platform

#### Instagram
- Design process videos
- Team spotlights
- Work showcases
- Community features

#### Twitter/X
- Design opinions
- Tool recommendations
- Industry commentary
- Quick tips

#### LinkedIn
- Company milestones
- Hiring announcements
- Long-form insights
- Case study highlights

### Visual Territories

| Territory | Use Case | Visual Style |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **Auto** | Performance content | High-contrast, dramatic |
| **Lifestyle** | Human connection | Authentic, natural light |
| **Move** | Dynamic content | Motion blur, energy |
| **Work** | Case studies | Before/after, data |
| **Escape** | Inspiration | Environmental, golden hour |
| **Feel** | Abstract | Textures, gradients |

### Post Guidelines

| Element | Guideline |
|---------|-----------|
| **Captions** | Conversational, value-first |
| **Hashtags** | Minimal, relevant (3-5 max) |
| **Frequency** | Consistent over volume |
| **Engagement** | Respond authentically |

## Thumbnails

Guidelines for video thumbnails and preview images.

### Composition Rules

| Rule | Reason |
|------|--------|
| **Face Forward** | Human faces increase engagement |
| **High Contrast** | Stands out in feeds |
| **Minimal Text** | 3-5 words maximum |
| **Clear Subject** | One focal point |

### Typography Treatment

- **Font**: OffBit or NH Display Bold
- **Size**: Large enough to read at small sizes
- **Placement**: Lower third or centered
- **Color**: High contrast with background

### Color Application

| Background | Text Color | Accent |
|------------|------------|--------|
| Charcoal | Vanilla | Aperol sparingly |
| Vanilla | Charcoal | Aperol sparingly |
| Photography | Overlay + contrasting text | — |

### Thumbnail Types

| Type | Treatment |
|------|-----------|
| **Tutorial** | Face + topic text |
| **Talk/Interview** | Speaker face, event branding |
| **Process** | Work preview + title |
| **Announcement** | Bold text, minimal imagery |

## Places & Spaces

Physical and environmental brand activation.

### Event Presence

| Element | Guidelines |
|---------|------------|
| **Booth Design** | Charcoal/Vanilla palette, minimal |
| **Signage** | Logo at proper scale, clear hierarchy |
| **Swag** | Useful items, quality materials |
| **Team Dress** | Casual professional, brand colors optional |

### Environmental Design

| Space Type | Application |
|------------|-------------|
| **Office** | Warm, creative, functional |
| **Meeting Rooms** | Named, themed, equipped |
| **Common Areas** | Community-focused, comfortable |
| **External Events** | Portable brand elements |

### Signage Principles

- **Scale**: Logo minimum sizes apply (110px digital, 70px small)
- **Placement**: Eye-level, unobstructed
- **Materials**: Quality over quantity
- **Lighting**: Consider visibility conditions

### Merchandise Guidelines

| Category | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| **Apparel** | Simple, wearable, quality fabrics |
| **Accessories** | Useful (notebooks, bags, bottles) |
| **Stickers** | Brand marks, taglines, illustrations |
| **Print** | Limited runs, thoughtful design |

### Production Standards

- **Materials**: Sustainable when possible
- **Quality**: Items people want to keep
- **Branding**: Subtle over loud
- **Packaging**: Considered, minimal waste
